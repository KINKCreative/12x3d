ABOUT THE PROJECT

12x3D is a project by a Los Angeles based collective of filmmakers. It’s essentially a series of 12 short 3-5 minute films of various genres. The idea is to challenge oneself as filmmakers in writing, producing and directing each of the main genres. It’s a filmmaking experiment in a desire to explore the challenges and the rewards of a genre and have tons of fun doing it.

PRODUCTION

All the projects are planned for execution in 1 or 2 days at most, over a weekend. They would be shot in Cinematic VR, whether in 2D or 3D depending on the equipment we would be able to procure.

A production company would join the project with support, e.g. providing equipment, consulting and whatever else would be in its capacity and willingness to contribute. The series would be self produced.

APPROACH AND TIMELINE

The collective gathers loglines from any participant who wishes to submit one or more. The collective would vote on the loglines to come up with a list of projects to produce.

The next round’s genre is pulled out of a hat, shot and produced and published online. We would continue to gather loglines for the remaining genres  as the project progresses.
